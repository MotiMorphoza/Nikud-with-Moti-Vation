How it works

  1. 1 No Nikud yet? Open DICTA and vocalize your Hebrew text there first.
  2. 2 Paste Use the Paste button or Ctrl+V to drop the vocalized text in.
  3. 3 Read The tool instantly strips complex marks — only clear, learner-friendly vowels remain.
  4. 4 Copy Hit Copy and paste the result anywhere.
